Welcome to the Department of Computer Science at Addis Ababa University

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science at Addis Ababa University! We are delighted to have you explore our vibrant academic community within the College of Natural and Computational Sciences. Our department offers a comprehensive and rigorous 4-year undergraduate program designed to equip students with the essential knowledge and skills needed to excel in the ever-evolving field of computer science.

In this department, students delve into various core areas of study including programming languages, algorithms, software engineering, computer systems and architecture, artificial intelligence, machine learning, database systems, human-computer interaction, and cybersecurity. Our curriculum is carefully crafted to provide a balanced mix of theoretical foundations and practical applications, ensuring that our graduates are well-prepared for both professional careers and advanced studies.

Join us in exploring the fascinating world of computer science, where innovation meets opportunity. Our dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art facilities are here to support you every step of the way, fostering an environment of learning, research, and growth. We look forward to embarking on this exciting journey with you at Addis Ababa University's Department of Computer Science.
