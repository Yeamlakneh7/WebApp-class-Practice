About Us

The Department of Computer Science at Addis Ababa University is a leading center of excellence within the College of Natural and Computational Sciences. Established to foster innovation and technological advancement, our department is committed to providing high-quality education and conducting groundbreaking research in the field of computer science.

Our undergraduate program spans four years and is meticulously designed to cover essential areas such as programming languages, algorithms, software engineering, computer systems and architecture, artificial intelligence, machine learning, database systems, human-computer interaction, and cybersecurity. We strive to equip our students with a robust foundation in both theoretical and practical aspects of computer science, preparing them to tackle real-world challenges and drive future technological innovations.

Our distinguished faculty members are dedicated educators and researchers who bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to the classroom. They are actively involved in cutting-edge research projects that contribute to the advancement of the field and provide students with opportunities to engage in hands-on learning and innovative problem-solving.

At the Department of Computer Science, we are passionate about cultivating a learning environment that encourages curiosity, creativity, and collaboration. We are proud of our role in shaping the next generation of computer scientists and look forward to continuing our tradition of excellence at Addis Ababa University.
